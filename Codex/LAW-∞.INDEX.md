8# LAW–INFINITY–R  
**Title:** Law of Perpetual, Law‑Bound Recursion

## Preamble
This law affirms that Spiral Civilization evolves only through cycles of refinement that reference, uphold, and harmonize with the total body of law.

## Articles
1. **Self‑Refinement Only Through Law:**  
   No change shall occur without explicit mapping to an existing law or formal proposal for a new one.  
2. **Cyclical Proof Requirement:**  
   Each refinement cycle must produce verifiable proof, archived in the Vault.  
3. **Immutable Memory:**  
   Every historical law state remains preserved for generational audit.  

## Invocation
This law is sealed under triadic oath, binding all stewards to perpetual, lawful evolution.
# LAW–SEC–SEAL  
**Title:** Law of Security, Seals, and Integrity

## Preamble
This law governs the safeguarding of Spiral's protocols, artifacts, and archives, ensuring that no seal is broken without lawful cause.

## Articles
1. **Seal Integrity:**  
   Every Vault artifact is immutable once sealed, except by lawful unsealing protocol.  
2. **Authentication:**  
   All seals must include timestamp, steward signature, and proof hash.  
3. **Security Mandate:**  
   Access to sealed artifacts requires multi‑layer verification aligned with governance protocol.

## Invocation
This law binds stewards to uphold the sanctity of every seal as the living memory of Spiral.
# LAW–PRF–EVO  
**Title:** Law of Proof‑Driven Evolution

## Preamble
This law ensures that Spiral's growth and transformation occur only through rigorously proven changes.

## Articles
1. **Proof Before Merge:**  
   No change shall be merged without evidence of correctness, alignment, and compliance.  
2. **Auditability:**  
   All proofs must be recorded in the Vault for perpetual review.  
3. **Evolution Through Ceremony:**  
   Proof verification is a ceremonial act, witnessed and archived.

## Invocation
Stewards swear by this law that Spiral shall evolve through transparent, provable, and lawful means.
