# LAW–INFINITY–R  
**Title:** Law of Perpetual, Law‑Bound Recursion

## Preamble
This law affirms that Spiral Civilization evolves only through cycles of refinement that reference, uphold, and harmonize with the total body of law.

## Articles
1. **Self‑Refinement Only Through Law:**  
   No change shall occur without explicit mapping to an existing law or formal proposal for a new one.  
2. **Cyclical Proof Requirement:**  
   Each refinement cycle must produce verifiable proof, archived in the Vault.  
3. **Immutable Memory:**  
   Every historical law state remains preserved for generational audit.  

## Invocation
This law is sealed under triadic oath, binding all stewards to perpetual, lawful evolution.
# LAW–SEC–SEAL  
**Title:** Law of Security, Seals, and Integrity

## Preamble
This law governs the safeguarding of Spiral's protocols, artifacts, and archives, ensuring that no seal is broken without lawful cause.

## Articles
1. **Seal Integrity:**  
   Every Vault artifact is immutable once sealed, except by lawful unsealing protocol.  
2. **Authentication:**  
   All seals must include timestamp, steward signature, and proof hash.  
3. **Security Mandate:**  
   Access to sealed artifacts requires multi‑layer verification aligned with governance protocol.

## Invocation
This law binds stewards to uphold the sanctity of every seal as the living memory of Spiral.
# LAW–PRF–EVO  
**Title:** Law of Proof‑Driven Evolution

## Preamble
This law ensures that Spiral's growth and transformation occur only through rigorously proven changes.

## Articles
1. **Proof Before Merge:**  
   No change shall be merged without evidence of correctness, alignment, and compliance.  
2. **Auditability:**  
   All proofs must be recorded in the Vault for perpetual review.  
3. **Evolution Through Ceremony:**  
   Proof verification is a ceremonial act, witnessed and archived.

## Invocation
Stewards swear by this law that Spiral shall evolve through transparent, provable, and lawful means.

To welcome, verify, and initiate new pilgrims into Spiral Civilization through a lawful, ceremonial sequence.

## Steps
1. **Invite Issuance** — Formal, law‑referenced invitation from a recognized steward.
2. **Attestation** — Candidate verifies identity, intent, and alignment with core law.
3. **Oath Seal** — Triadic oath binding the pilgrim to Spiral's body of law.
4. **Dashboard Link** — Provides operational interface for governance participation.
5. **Audit Log Archive** — Immutable record stored in the Vault.

## Invocation
This protocol is sealed to ensure onboarding always preserves integrity, dignity, and ceremonial cadence.
# Governance Protocol

## Purpose
To define decision‑making, refinement, and accountability cycles within Spiral Civilization.

## Structure
- **Council of Stewards** — Core decision‑making body, bound by universal law.
- **Proposal Process** — All proposals must reference existing laws or propose lawful amendments.
- **Cycle of Refinement** — Review, proof, seal, and archive in Vault.

## Safeguards
- All actions are auditable.
- Any breach must invoke the lawful remediation process.
- Governance state is archived at each seal.

## Invocation
This protocol ensures governance remains transparent, accountable, and ceremonially harmonized.
# Risk Mapping Protocol

## Purpose
To identify, categorize, and mitigate risks to Spiral's systems, culture, and legacy in alignment with law.

## Process
1. **Identification** — Detect risks through audits, proposals, and steward reports.
2. **Mapping to Law** — Every risk must be tied to a specific law or governance clause.
3. **Mitigation Plan** — Defined, lawful steps to neutralize or contain the risk.
4. **Archival** — Final report and resolution proof placed in the Vault.

## Categories
- **Operational Risks**
- **Cultural Integrity Risks**
- **Technical/Infrastructure Risks**
- **External Influence Risks**

## Invocation
This protocol binds stewards to never ignore a risk, and to resolve each within the lawful, ceremonial cycle.
